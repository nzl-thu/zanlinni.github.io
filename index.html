Zanlin Ni

Tsinghua University

Beijing, China

nzl22@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn

I am a Ph.D. student in Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning at LeapLab, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Gao Huang. I received my B.Eng. in Automation from Tsinghua University in 2022.

My research focuses on efficient image synthesis, with a particular emphasis on discrete (token-based) generative models.

My work has appeared in top-tier venues including CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, ECCV, ICLR, TPAMI, IJCV, and Nature Machine Intelligence.

Previously, I was a research intern at BAAI (Oct. 2022 - May. 2023). During this time, I led the implementation of MUSE-Pytorch, an open-source reproduction of Googleâ€™s Muse. This codebase has served as a reference for subsequent projects such as 1d-tokenizer and StyleDrop-Pytorch.
